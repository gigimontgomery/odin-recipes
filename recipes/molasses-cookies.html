Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup vegetable oil/li>
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ½ tsp ground cloves
  • ⅓ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 ° F (180° C)
  2. In a large bowl mix oil and 1 cup of sugar. Add egg, beat well. Stir in molasses. @ cups flour, baking soda and spices. If necessary, add more flour to make a firm dough.
  3. Shape dough into 1 ¼ inch balls. Roll in ⅓ cup sugar. Place 3 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake 12-14 minutes or until tops crack. Remove from baking sheet and cool on rack.
